Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What is the purity level of your peptides?

All our peptides undergo rigorous third-party testing to ensure purity levels of 99% or higher. Each batch comes with a Certificate of Analysis (COA) that details the purity, potency, and sterility results.

How do you ensure product quality and consistency?

We implement strict quality control measures throughout our manufacturing process, including HPLC analysis, mass spectrometry verification, and sterility testing. Our peptides are produced in cGMP-compliant facilities and every batch is independently verified.

What shipping methods do you use?

We use temperature-controlled shipping with insulated packaging and ice packs to maintain product integrity during transit. All shipments are discreetly packaged without any external branding that identifies the contents.

Do you offer research consultation services?

Yes, we offer research consultation services through our team of scientific experts. They can provide guidance on peptide selection, research protocols, and application methods based on your specific research objectives.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns of unopened products within 30 days of purchase. Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns of opened items unless there is a verified quality issue. Please contact our customer service team for assistance with returns.

How should peptides be stored?

Peptides should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct light. For long-term storage, we recommend keeping them in a freezer at -20°C. Once reconstituted, peptides should be refrigerated and used within the recommended timeframe.
